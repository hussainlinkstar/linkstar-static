Frequenty asked questions

How long does it take for an international transfer to be completed?

Our service states that international transfers can take up to 3 days. However, processing time can vary depending on the destination and the service provider.

What information does my client need to provide for an international payment?

They will need their debit/credit card details and your Linkstar portfolio link. Yup, that’s all!

Are there any fees associated with international payments?

Yes, fees may apply. Linkstar charges a fixed amount of 3.5% on each transaction. However, the amount may vary depending on the service provider and the specific terms of your transfer. It's advisable to check with your bank for detailed information on fees.

Are there any restrictions on the amount I can transfer internationally?

Linkstar requires you to make a minimum payment of $5.00 through the wallet. There may be limits on the amount you can transfer, depending on the service provider and the destination country. Check with your provider for specific limits.

What is the exchange rate used for international transfers?

Exchange rates can fluctuate and are determined by the service provider. It's advisable to check the rate at the time of the transfer and be aware of any additional fees.
